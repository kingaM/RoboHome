1. URL

http://www.robohome.com/ip/version/(string:versionNo)/rooms/(int:roomId)/(string: type)s/(int:itemId / all)/(string:action)?(params)

Example

http://www.robohome.com/0.0.0.0/version/0.1/rooms/1/doors/1/setOpen?percentage=50
